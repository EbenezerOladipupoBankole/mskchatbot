Musik-Assist

Your intelligent music assistant powered by Gemini AI

What is Musik-Assist?

Musik-Assist is an AI-powered chatbot designed to help you with all things music-related. Whether you're looking for song recommendations, artist information, lyrics, or just want to talk about music, Musik-Assist is here to help.

Features

  • Ask anything related to music — genres, artists, songs, albums
  • Get recommendations based on your preferences
  • Chat in guest mode or log in for unlimited access
  • Powered by Google Gemini AI for accurate and helpful responses

Who Made This?

Musik-Assist was created by Ebenezer Musik Assitantal as a personal project to combine the power of AI with music knowledge. It's built using Python, Flask, AWS DynamoDB, and the Google Gemini API.
